Linuxers for Obama

21 April 2008

Linuxers for Obama – LinuxersForObama is a campaign for all Free and Open Source Software advocates to contribute to the Obama campaign. Sorry for the shorthand “LinuxForObama”, I know it should have been “FLOSSGNUX11BSDApacheMySQLForObama”, but I went for short and simple.

Hopefully team LinuxForObama can do some nice fund raising for Obama and we can settle once and for all the argument about which operating system is the best.

I just donated a bit. I have never, ever done anything like this. I have been pondering, and thinking on doing this time, until this morning when George sent me this link through GTalk.

Do your little bit too, please. Click on the link, donate! Alternatively, you could Digg it.
