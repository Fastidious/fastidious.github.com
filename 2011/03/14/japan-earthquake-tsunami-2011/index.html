Japan Earthquake and Tsunami

14 March 2011

Japan’s destruction due to the recent earthquake and tsunami is unbelievable. It is more than heartbreaking. Google has setup a page (Google’s Japan Earthquake Crisis Response) with informational links, one of them being one to donate through the Red Cross Japan. Around the clock information from the BBC is also available.

A small donation can go a long way. I have donated, and you can too. Please help by donating (Google’s Japan Earthquake Crisis Response) today!
