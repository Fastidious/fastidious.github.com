Three in less than a month

10 September 2004

There hasn’t been that much movement around here –on the weblog, I mean–, since hurricanes have kept us busy during the second half of August and the first of September. First Charley, then Frances and now Ivan. The stress level of people around is pretty high, so is their worry. There is a saying, you know, “three and you are out!”. Everyone I know, whose house or properties have been undamaged, is thinking on that saying.

I will see you all after the hurricane. Oh, on a separate note, I am testing posting to the weblog via a weblog client (Windows based application). Let’s see how it comes out.
