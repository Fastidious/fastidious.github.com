First day

11 July 2004

I must admit, before I continue, that I did not exercise last night. At least, not as I had intended. It is my fault, I became lazy and even though Kim pushed me well, it did not worked out. I will be fit in no time. I have to. I must. Next, I will buy a pedometer. Thanks to my friend Martin for the suggestion!
